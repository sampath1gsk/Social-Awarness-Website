Quit Smoking

Quit-smoking products fall into two main categories:
  • Nicotine replacement product like:
    • patches
    • gum
    • spray
    • inhaler...Although some are available without a prescription, it's best to talk with your doctor before trying them.

  • Two medications that don't contain nicotine are available by prescription.
    • Nicotine patch
    • Nicotine gum
    • Nicotine nasal spray
These are the 2 best medications helps to quit smoking.
